Caitlin loves creating images that make the viewer feel as if they are stepping into a dream. She is a portrait and commercial photographer residing in Melbourne, VIC, Australia. She began her journey through photography at the age of 15. She then wen't on to study for 3 years at the Central Institute of Technology, graduating with an Advanced Diploma in Photography in 2009. Caitlin hopes to help create breathtaking images for all mediums be, commercial, domestic, creative + fashion.
